How to Buy ?

Create a Wallet

download phantom wallet or wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension

Get Some SOL

have SOL in your wallet to switch to $unstable. If you don't have any SOL, you can buy directly on phantom, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

Go to photon

connect to photon. Go to photon-sol.tinyastro.io in google chrome or on the browser inside your phantom app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $unstable address into phantom wallet, select sol, and confirm. When prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.
